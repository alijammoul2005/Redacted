Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I register for an account?

    +

    To register, visit the Citizen Portal and click on "Register". You'll need your National ID, email address, and phone number. Fill in the required information, accept the terms and conditions, and submit. You'll receive a confirmation email to activate your account.

  • How long does it take to process a document request?

    +

    Processing times vary depending on the type of document. Most standard documents are processed within 3-5 business days. Complex requests may take up to 10 business days. You can track your request status in the Citizen Portal dashboard.

  • What payment methods are accepted?

    +

    We accept credit cards (Visa, Mastercard), debit cards, bank transfers, and e-wallets. All online payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment gateway. You can also pay in person at our office during business hours.

  • How can I track my complaint?

    +

    After submitting a complaint through the Citizen Portal, you'll receive a complaint ID. Log in to your dashboard to view the status, updates, and any responses from our staff. You'll also receive email notifications when your complaint status changes.

  • Can I cancel or modify a request after submission?

    +

    Requests in "Submitted" status can be cancelled through your dashboard. Once a request moves to "Under Review" or beyond, you'll need to contact us directly to make changes. Please call our support line or visit our office during business hours.

  • What should I do if I forgot my password?

    +

    Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.

  • How do I report an emergency or urgent issue?

    +

    For emergencies, please call our emergency hotline at +961 XX XXX XXX (available 24/7). For urgent but non-emergency issues, you can submit a high-priority complaint through the Citizen Portal, marking it as "Urgent". Our team monitors urgent submissions and responds within 24 hours.

  • Are my personal information and documents secure?

    +

    Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. All information is stored securely and accessed only by authorized personnel. We comply with data protection regulations and never share your information with third parties without your consent. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.
