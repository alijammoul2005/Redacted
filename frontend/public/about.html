About Our Municipality

Serving our community with dedication, transparency, and innovation since 1950

Our Mission

To provide exceptional public services to our citizens through efficient administration, sustainable development, and community engagement. We are committed to enhancing the quality of life for all residents while preserving our community's heritage and promoting responsible growth.

Our Vision

To be a model municipality known for innovation, transparency, and citizen-centric services. We envision a thriving, sustainable community where every resident has access to quality municipal services and opportunities for participation in local governance.
